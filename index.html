Mannequinn


Mannequinn is a responsive web application built using Ruby on Rails. It is a fashion rental service that offer's members the chance to rent or loan items from/ to one and other. It was designed, built and shipped to development by a team of three in just 2 weeks.

I have grown increasingly conscious of the effect that fast fashion has on the planet and on a social level too. We have a responsibility to the planet and people to cut down and start reusing what we already have. I found that the market was lacking a user to user app where people could rent clothes from each other and rent out their wardrobes too. Why not save the planet and make save yourself some cash at the same time? It's a win-win situation in my eyes. This is where the Mannequinn prototype comes in.


As a team we mapped out the core user journey and decided on the main values we wated to portray in the design.

  • Style
  • Sustainability
  • Trust

We all played a part in the back-end development of the site meaning the site boasts numerous functional features including:

  • User registration / Login
  • Product creation
  • Chat service
  • Favorites
  • Booking service
  • Booking requests
  • Product filtering and sorting
  • Payment gateway (Stripe)
